Frequently Asked Question (FAQ)

1. How does your meal delivery service work?

Answer: Our meal delivery service is designed to make healthy eating convenient and hassle-free. Simply choose your desired meal plan, place your order through our website or contact us directly, and we'll take care of the rest. Our team will prepare fresh, nutritious meals according to your preferences and dietary requirements and deliver them to your doorstep on your scheduled delivery days.

2. Where do you deliver?

Answer: We currently offer delivery services to Kuala Lumpur area. Our delivery area includes Setapak and Gombak. If you're unsure whether we deliver to your location, please feel free to contact us, and we'll be happy to assist you.

3. What types of meal plans do you offer?

Answer: We offer a variety of meal plans to suit different dietary preferences and lifestyles, including standard balanced meals, vegetarian options, low-carb or keto-friendly meals, and more. Whether you're looking to lose weight, maintain a healthy lifestyle, or simply save time on meal preparation, we have a plan that's right for you.

4. Are your meals customizable?

Answer: Yes, we understand that everyone's tastes and dietary needs are unique. That's why we offer customization options for our meal plans. You can specify any dietary restrictions, allergies, or food preferences when placing your order, and our chefs will tailor your meals accordingly. We also provide the flexibility to make changes to your meal selections or delivery schedule as needed.
